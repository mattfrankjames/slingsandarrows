How to Install

Chrome / Edge (Android & Desktop)

Tap the install banner above, or open the browser menu (⋮) and choose "Install app".

Safari (iOS / iPadOS)

Tap the Share button (↗), then tap "Add to Home Screen", then tap Add.

Firefox

Open the browser menu (⋯) and tap "Install" or "Add to Home Screen".

Safari (macOS)

In the menu bar choose File → Add to Dock (macOS Sonoma+).
